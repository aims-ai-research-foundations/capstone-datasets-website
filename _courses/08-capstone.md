## About This Course

The capstone project is your opportunity to apply everything you've learned to solve a real problem. You'll work on a project relevant to African contexts and present your work at a showcase event.

## Project Themes

Choose a theme that addresses challenges in your country or region:

- **Healthcare**: Disease prediction, health information systems
- **Agriculture**: Crop yield prediction, pest detection
- **Finance**: Fraud detection, financial inclusion
- **Education**: Adaptive learning, content generation
- **Climate**: Weather prediction, environmental monitoring
- **Governance**: Public service optimization, transparency tools
- **Language Technology**: Translation, NER, sentiment analysis

## Capstone Requirements

1. **Problem Definition**: Clear problem statement with measurable goals
2. **Dataset**: Use curated datasets from this catalog or collect your own
3. **Solution**: End-to-end implementation using course techniques
4. **Evaluation**: Rigorous evaluation with appropriate metrics
5. **Responsible AI**: Document ethical considerations and limitations
6. **Presentation**: Showcase your work at the HEI event

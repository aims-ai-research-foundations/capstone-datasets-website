## About Capstone Datasets

Capstone Datasets is a curated collection of datasets designed specifically for AI Research Foundations capstone projects. This project is part of the **Africa AI Upskilling Initiative**, which aims to build a sustainable and scalable ecosystem for advanced AI education across Higher Educational Institutions (HEIs) in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, and South Africa.

## The Africa AI Upskilling Initiative

Through a **Train-the-Trainer (TtT)** model, the initiative supports HEI lecturers (AI Champions) with the skills to deliver a localized version of the Google DeepMind curriculum to penultimate and final-year STEM students.

### Key Statistics

| Metric | Number |
|--------|--------|
| Countries | 4 (Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa) |
| Higher Education Institutions | 30 |
| AI Champions (Lecturers) | 146 |
| Teaching Assistants | 146+ |
| Students (projected) | 31,000+ |

### The Curriculum

The Google DeepMind AI Research Foundations curriculum comprises 8 courses:

1. **Build Your Own Small Language Model**
2. **Represent Your Language Data**
3. **Design and Train Neural Networks**
4. **Assemble Your Transformer Architecture**
5. **Finetune Your Model**
6. **Align Your Model**
7. **Accelerate Your Model**
8. **Capstone: Develop Your Model for Real-World Impact**

## Data Hosting Policy

This website is a **dataset catalogue**, not a dataset hosting service. We provide:

- Curated links to high-quality, accessible datasets
- Clear download and usage instructions (Colab-ready)
- Structured metadata (languages, countries, tasks, licenses)
- Dataset cards with provenance, ethics, and limitations
- Course-to-dataset mappings

We do **NOT** host large datasets directly. Instead, we link to canonical sources (Hugging Face, Zenodo, government portals, research repositories) to ensure:

- Legal compliance with dataset licenses
- Access to the latest versions
- Proper attribution to original creators

## About Capstone Datasets

Capstone Datasets is a curated collection of datasets designed specifically for AI Research Foundations capstone projects. This project is part of the **Africa AI Upskilling Initiative**, a landmark collaboration aiming to democratize access to advanced artificial intelligence research capabilities across Sub-Saharan Africa.

## The Africa AI Upskilling Initiative

The initiative is a multi-year, multi-country program designed to bridge the critical AI skills gap in Africa. Moving beyond basic digital literacy, the program focuses on deep, technical research proficiency—empowering African students to transition from consumers of AI technology to creators and researchers.

Through a **Train-the-Trainer (TtT)** model, the initiative supports university lecturers (AI Champions) with the skills to deliver a thoughtfully localized version of the Google DeepMind curriculum to penultimate and final-year STEM students.

### Program Overview

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Duration | 3 Years |
| Countries | 4 (Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa) |
| Higher Education Institutions | 30 |
| AI Champions (Lecturers) | 292 |
| Students | 30,900 |

### The Curriculum

The **AI Research Foundations** curriculum is a rigorous, eight-course series co-developed by Google DeepMind and UCL. It guides learners from the fundamental principles of language modeling through to the complexities of Transformer architectures, model alignment, and real-world deployment:

1. **Build Your Own Small Language Model** - Fundamentals of language modeling and text generation
2. **Represent Your Language Data** - Tokenization, embeddings, and data representation
3. **Design and Train Neural Networks** - Deep learning mechanics and training
4. **Assemble Your Transformer Architecture** - Attention mechanisms and modern architectures
5. **Finetune Your Model** - Transfer learning and domain adaptation
6. **Align Your Model** - Safety, bias mitigation, and responsible AI
7. **Accelerate Your Model** - Optimization and deployment
8. **Capstone: Develop Your Model for Real-World Impact** - End-to-end project

## Responsible AI at the Core

A distinguishing feature of this program is the deep integration of **Responsible AI** throughout the curriculum. Rather than treating ethics as a separate module, concepts of safety, bias, and alignment are woven into the technical fabric of every course. Students learn to:

- Use **Data Cards** to document datasets transparently
- Anticipate the societal impact of their models
- Implement technical methods for mitigating toxicity and bias
- Conduct safety evaluations and "Red Teaming"
- Design AI systems that are helpful, harmless, and honest

This ensures that the next generation of African AI researchers builds technology responsibly from day one.

## The Capstone Project

The Capstone Project is the defining element of the AI Research Foundations program—the "ultimate test" of a learner's understanding, requiring students to act as independent researchers.

### Requirements

Students identify a specific, tangible problem and develop an AI-backed solution that demonstrates:

1. **Problem Identification** - Selecting a challenge that is relevant and solvable
2. **Data Strategy** - Curating and representing data ethically
3. **Model Selection & Training** - Choosing the right architecture and training approach
4. **Alignment & Safety** - Ensuring the solution is ethical and unbiased
5. **Optimization** - Making the solution deployable in real-world conditions

### Capstone Project Areas

The program encourages projects with real-world impact in the African context:

| Area | Example Applications |
|------|---------------------|
| **Low-Resource Language Translation** | Models for Yoruba, Swahili, Twi—preserving cultural heritage |
| **Agricultural Intelligence** | Crop yield prediction, plant disease identification |
| **Healthcare Diagnostics** | Disease diagnosis optimized for mobile devices |
| **Climate Resilience** | Weather prediction, flood risk analysis |
| **Financial Inclusion** | Credit scoring for unbanked populations |
| **Education** | Personalized learning tools for African students |

The Capstone bridges undergraduate study and advanced research careers, validating that students possess the practical engineering capability to drive technological progress.

## Data Hosting Policy

This website is a **dataset catalogue**, not a dataset hosting service. We provide:

- Curated links to high-quality, accessible datasets
- Clear download and usage instructions (Colab-ready)
- Structured metadata (languages, countries, tasks, licenses)
- Dataset cards with provenance, ethics, and limitations
- Course-to-dataset mappings

We link to canonical sources (Hugging Face, Zenodo, government portals, research repositories) to ensure legal compliance, access to the latest versions, and proper attribution to original creators.
